
Common online shopping scams and red flags:
- Prices that are too good to be true, like 70% or 80% off brand new electronics
- Sellers with no reviews or very new accounts
- Fake discount scams where price is hiked before sale
- AI generated reviews that use repeated phrases
- Sellers not offering return policy
- Unverified third party sellers on major platforms
- Counterfeit products listed as original
- No contact information or customer support provided
- Pressure tactics like "only 1 left" or "offer ends in 1 hour"
- Requests for payment outside the platform
